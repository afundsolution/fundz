# Personal Phone Redirect SOP

Purpose: protect Brandon's personal line while making sure existing A FUND Solution members know where to communicate.

## Rule

Personal phone is an intake signal only. It is not the official client support channel.

Do not auto-reply from Brandon's personal phone. Do not copy personal-phone message bodies into Google Sheets, Slack, or shared reports without fresh Brandon approval.

## Existing Member Redirect

Use this when the person is an existing member and the approved response channel is available:

```text
Hi {first_name}, for anything related to your A FUND Solution file, please message us through your Credit Tracker app. That keeps your updates, questions, and next steps in the right place so the team can track everything clearly.
```

If they need help finding the app:

```text
Hi {first_name}, please use your Credit Tracker app for file questions and updates going forward. If you cannot get into the app, reply through the company channel and we can help with access.
```

## Unknown Or Personal

If the row is not clearly an existing member, do not redirect automatically. Keep it as review/no-company-action unless Brandon confirms it is business-related.

## Work Queue Gate

A personal-phone item becomes company work only when it has:

- owner
- status
- next step
- proof requirement
- evidence
- approved company response channel

## Never Do From This Lane

- Do not send from the personal line automatically.
- Do not tell a client to keep using Brandon's personal line.
- Do not verify DF/HighLevel unless the row is business-related and approved for work.
- Do not move personal rows into the shared Work Queue.
